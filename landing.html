METHODOLOGY
How it works
From raw ufcstats.com career averages to a calibrated prediction in under 2 seconds.
01
Feature engineering
12 differentials from career averages — striking output, accuracy, absorption, defense, takedown stats, submission rate, and composite edges.
02
XGBoost + Optuna
Gradient-boosted trees tuned via 50-trial Bayesian optimization. Each fight appears twice — once per fighter as F1 — symmetric balanced dataset.
03
SHAP analysis
TreeExplainer computes per-feature Shapley values. Net strike edge dominates — 2x the importance of the next feature.
04
JS surrogate
Logistic regression fit on XGBoost predictions achieves 89.3% agreement — real-time predictions with no backend lag.
