METHODOLOGY
How it works
From raw ufcstats data to a calibrated prediction in under 2 seconds.
01
Feature engineering
12 differentials computed from career averages — striking output, accuracy, absorption, defense, takedown stats, submission rate, and composite edges.
02
XGBoost with Optuna
Gradient-boosted trees tuned via Bayesian optimization. Each fight appears twice — once per fighter as F1 — creating a symmetric balanced dataset.
03
SHAP analysis
TreeExplainer computes per-feature Shapley values. Net strike edge dominates — 2x the importance of any other feature.
04
JS surrogate model
Logistic regression fit on XGBoost's predictions achieves 89.3% agreement — enabling real-time predictions in the browser with no backend call.
